What the ICI Is

A 90-minute session designed to repair or establish the foundation everything else must be built on.

The ICI is not therapy. It is not a vision board session. It is not motivational content dressed up as coaching.

It is a structured, faith-centered deep-dive that takes you through five sequential movements — each one excavating a different layer of who you are beneath the roles, the pressure, and the performance.

By the time we close, you will have five Truth Statements that become your Identity Foundation — a written declaration of who you are, what you stand for, where you have been fractured, what lies you are leaving behind, and who God is in your life.

This is Phase I of the CORE Alignment Method™. And nothing sustainable gets built until this phase is complete.

Identity Recalibration Who you are versus what you do — separating the permanent from the temporary

05
Faith as Foundation Re-centering God at the core of your identity — not the margin of your life
